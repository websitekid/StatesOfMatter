States of matter

Distinct forms of matter that you see every day including solids, liquids, gases, and plasma.

Solid

The state of matter with a deffinate volume and a definte shape

Liquid

The state of matter that has a definate volume but takes the place of the solid its put in

Gas

The state of matter that has no shape or no volume.

Plasma

A state of matter that is similar to gas but instead has ions and free electrons
